Using the App
How do I add an expense?▸
Tap the + button in the centre of the tab bar to add a new expense. Enter the amount, choose a category, and optionally add a name and date.
How do I edit or delete an expense?▸
Tap on any expense in your recent activity or spending list to edit it. You can also swipe left on an expense in the Spending tab to delete it.
What's the difference between Needs and Wants?▸
Needs are essential expenses like rent, utilities, and groceries. Wants are discretionary spending like entertainment or dining out. Categorising helps you understand your spending habits.
How do I create custom categories?▸
When adding an expense, tap 'Choose Category' and then tap the + button to create a new category. You can pick an icon and colour for each category.
How do recurring expenses work?▸
When adding an expense, toggle 'Recurring' and choose a frequency (daily, weekly, fortnightly, monthly, or yearly). The app will automatically log the expense on each due date. Manage your recurring expenses from Settings > Recurring Expenses.
Does the app have widgets?▸
Yes! Long-press your Home Screen, tap the + button, and search for Every Bit Counts. There are four widgets: Today's Spending, Budget Ring, Weekly Summary, and Quick Add for logging expenses without opening the app.
Does my data sync across devices?▸
Yes. Your expenses sync automatically via iCloud when you're signed in with the same Apple ID. Changes on one device will appear on your other devices shortly after.
Where is my data stored?▸
All your data is stored locally on your device and syncs via iCloud if you're signed in. We don't operate servers that store your financial information — your data stays private.
Subscription
What does the subscription include?▸
The subscription unlocks all premium features including the full tips library, advanced insights, and unlimited categories. It helps support ongoing development of the app.
How do I restore my subscription?▸
Go to Settings and tap 'Restore Subscription'. Sign in with the Apple ID you used to purchase, and your subscription will be restored. The app also attempts to restore your subscription automatically on each launch.
How do I manage or cancel my subscription?▸
Tap 'Manage Subscription' in Settings to open your Apple subscription settings. From there you can cancel, change plans, or update payment methods. You can also go to Settings > Apple Account > Subscriptions on your device.
Can I use my subscription on another device?▸
Yes. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID, not your device. Just sign in with the same Apple ID on your other device and your subscription will be restored automatically.
Will I lose my data if I cancel?▸
No. All your expenses and data remain on your device. You'll keep access to core features; only premium features will be locked.
Help & Settings
How do I change the currency?▸
Go to Settings > Preferences > Currency and select your preferred currency. Amounts throughout the app will update automatically.
How do I change the language?▸
Go to Settings > Preferences > Language and choose from 10 supported languages. The app detects your device language automatically on first install.
How do I change the app colour theme?▸
The app uses a green theme by default. Colour customisation is not currently available but may be added in a future update.
Can I replay the app tour?▸
Yes! Go to Settings and tap 'Replay Tutorial' to walk through the guided tour again at any time.
How do I delete all my data?▸
Go to Settings > Data > Delete All Data. This permanently removes all expenses, goals, and settings. This action cannot be undone.
